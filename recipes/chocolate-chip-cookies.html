Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is a simple homemade chocolate chip cookie recipe made by AllRecipes user Dora. It is highly rated by over 14,000 allrecipes users.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 tsp. hot water
  • 0.5 tsp. salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  2. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  3. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  4. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  7. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
